Microsoft 365 Administration

Modul orientat operational: ce configurezi, de ce configurezi, cum verifici.

Operationally focused module: what to configure, why, and how to validate.

1. Tenant Foundation

Teorie: tenantul este radacina organizationala pentru identitate, licentiere si servicii cloud.

Connect-MgGraph -Scopes Organization.Read.All,Domain.Read.All
Get-MgOrganization
Get-MgDomain

2. Identity & Access

Principiu: least privilege + grupuri administrative dedicate.

Get-MgUser -Top 20
Get-MgGroup -Top 20
Get-MgDirectoryRole

3. Licensing

Licentierea corecta evita costuri inutile si lipsa de servicii.

Get-MgSubscribedSku
Get-MgUserLicenseDetail -UserId user@contoso.com

4. Exchange Online

Connect-ExchangeOnline
Get-Mailbox -ResultSize 20
Get-MessageTrace -StartDate (Get-Date).AddDays(-1) -EndDate (Get-Date)

5. Teams

Configurezi colaborarea pe canale, meetings si external access.

Get-Team
Get-CsOnlineUser -ResultSize 20

6. SharePoint & OneDrive

Connect-SPOService -Url https://contoso-admin.sharepoint.com
Get-SPOSite -Limit 20

7. Compliance & DLP

Connect-IPPSSession
Get-DlpCompliancePolicy

8. Security Baseline

Get-MgIdentityConditionalAccessPolicy
